"AI is not replacing developers — it's giving them superpowers."

Modern development has shifted dramatically with the introduction of large language models capable of understanding and generating code. Teams that once spent days on boilerplate now ship features in hours.

The key insight is that AI excels at pattern completion, while humans excel at problem definition. Together, the combination is unbeatable.
